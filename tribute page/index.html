Biography

Yiliang "Peter" Peng or better known for his summoner name Doublelift, is the bot laner for Team Liquid. Peter hails from Mission Viejo, California and first discovered his love for video games through classic Nintendo 64 titles such as Star Fox and Golden Eye. After his brother got a computer, Peter would frequently play Diablo 1 or Starcraft. As soon as he got a computer of his own (passed down from his brother) he would spend more time playing games such as DotA, WoW, and HoN with his brothers and friends eventually leading him to his future career in League of Legends (LoL).
